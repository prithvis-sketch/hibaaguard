FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a scan take?
Most scans complete in 2–5 minutes depending on video length and complexity. You'll receive a full timestamped report as soon as the scan is complete — no waiting around.
Do you store my videos?
No. Videos are processed in isolated, temporary environments and deleted immediately after your report is generated. We do not retain video files, and we do not use your content to train any models.
Is this legal advice?
No. HIPAAGuard is a compliance screening tool, not a legal or regulatory advisory service. Our reports identify potential HIPAA risk indicators in your video content. For specific legal guidance, consult a qualified healthcare attorney or compliance officer.
Can I scan unpublished or draft content?
Yes — and that's exactly when you should scan. HIPAAGuard is designed to be used before you publish. Uploading raw or draft footage gives you the opportunity to address compliance issues before they ever reach a public platform.
Who is HIPAAGuard for?
HIPAAGuard is built for U.S. healthcare providers who create video content — physicians, dentists, orthodontists, med spa owners, surgical specialists, and the marketing teams that support them. If your practice posts clinical or educational video content online, HIPAAGuard is for you.
